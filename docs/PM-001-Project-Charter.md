# Vision

Build the world's first AI Enterprise Brain capable of understanding an industrial company exactly like its best experts.

Instead of searching documents, employees ask questions naturally and receive accurate, traceable, explainable answers.

PlantMind continuously learns from engineering knowledge, procedures, incidents, operations, maintenance history and corporate standards.

---

# Mission

Transform company knowledge into an intelligent operational asset.

The system becomes:

- Company Brain
- Digital Knowledge Twin
- Engineering Assistant
- Operations Advisor
- Decision Support Platform

---

# Core Objectives

1. Capture company knowledge.

2. Connect isolated information.

3. Reduce knowledge loss.

4. Accelerate troubleshooting.

5. Assist engineers.

6. Assist operators.

7. Improve safety.

8. Preserve organizational knowledge.
